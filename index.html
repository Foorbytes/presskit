About Foorbytes

Foorbytes is an indie game development company founded by Rob Foor, a passionate solo developer with a full-time job and three kids. The company serves as a creative outlet, focusing on crafting small, engaging games that resonate with players.

At Foorbytes, we are committed to creating original, fun experiences without relying on intrusive ads or cloning popular games. Our goal is to produce memorable and conversation-worthy games that leave a lasting impression on our players.

Our Philosophy

  • Create unique, engaging gameplay experiences
  • Focus on quality over quantity
  • Avoid intrusive monetization tactics
  • Prioritize player enjoyment and satisfaction
  • Continuously learn and improve our craft
