Press Kit - Bad Credit? No Problem!

Game Overview

"Bad Credit? No Problem!" is an indie game that immerses players in the world of a title loan agency, Title 4 Cash, set in 2016. As a new part-time employee, players must navigate a 14-day probation period in story mode, where they review and approve or deny loan applications based on an ever-evolving rule book.

Key Features

  • Immersive gameplay set in a title loan agency
  • Story mode with a 14-day probation period
  • Survival mode for extended gameplay
  • Meticulous paperwork inspection and decision-making
  • Dark humor and social commentary on the title loan industry
  • Pixel art graphics and immersive office sounds

Gameplay Details

Players start as a part-time employee at Title 4 Cash. They must review loan applications, checking details against an ever-changing rulebook. Success leads to full-time employment and unlocks the challenging survival mode. The game blends paperwork inspection, decision-making, and interactions with a greedy boss and a kind assistant.
